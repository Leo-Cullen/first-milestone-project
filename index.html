About

Who are we?

Established in 2006 in the British Midlands as LMC Solutions LTD, we are a small but thriving company. Our three engineers have thirty years of combined experience.

We pride ourselves on providing the highest levels of quality and service. Always willing to go the extra mile with competitive prices, our excellent references and full insuarance guarantee your peace of mind.

What do we do?

Projects that we have worked on range from individual houses to complex steel frame structures, pedestrian schemes, highways, concrete buildings, roads, car parks, sewage plants, schools and an athletics ground.

How do we do it?

We use Leica robotic survey equipment to collect, process and edit survey information. Together with our CAD expertise we can supply information in AutoCAD and other major formats.

Accuracy is paramount to us. Therefore, our strict quality quality assurance procedures ensure effective checking systems are in place at all stages of the production process.
